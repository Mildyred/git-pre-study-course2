Ingredients (probably not right...)

  • Soft butter, for greasing the pan
  • Flour, for dusting the buttered pan
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sugar, sifted
  • 1 cup brown sugar, sifted
  • 8 ounces melted butter
  • 2 cups cocoa, sifted
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup flour, sifted
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt

Instructions(may or may not be correct...)

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F.
  2. Butter and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  3. Combine and mix all the wet ingredients.
  4. Sieve and add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients.
  5. Pour the batter in an 8 inches pan and bake for 45 minutes.
  6. cut into cubes after completely cool
